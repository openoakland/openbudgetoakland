Who we are

Open Budget Oakland is the result of many contributors including coders, community advocates, and city officials. We're always looking for ideas and help, so get in touch and stop by our weekly meetup on Tuesdays 6–9:30pm at City Hall as part of OpenOakland hack night (send us an email ahead of time so we're sure to be available).
