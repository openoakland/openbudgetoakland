How would you use Open Budget?

We're trying to build a tool that's useful to all of Oakland — community advocates, journalists, city officials, and the people they serve. We want your ideas.

What part of the budget should be visualized, and how? What budget data should be accessible, and why? Who should be part of this discussion?

Add your ideas — and vote on others — in the comments below. The most popular ideas will guide the development of Open Budget.
